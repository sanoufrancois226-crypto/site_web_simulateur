Authors & Contributors

LoRaFlexSim was created by a team of researchers from the Department of Computer Science at Nazi Bony University and their collaborators. Their work is documented in the published paper “LoRaFlexSim: A Flexible LoRa/LoRaWAN Simulator”.

François d’Assise Sanou

PhD student at Nazi Bony University, Bobo‑Dioulasso, Burkina Faso. He leads the development of LoRaFlexSim and focuses on simulation fidelity and extensibility.

Personal website

Hamadoun Tall

Professor at Nazi Bony University. He contributes to the design of adaptive data rate algorithms and overall simulator architecture.

Gérard Chalhoub

Full professor at Clermont Auvergne University, France. He provides expertise in performance evaluation and simulation methodologies.

Tiguiane Yélémou

Full professor at Nazi Bony University. He oversees the project and guides research directions.

Most authors are affiliated with the Department of Computer Science, Nazi Bony University, Bobo‑Dioulasso, Burkina Faso. Gérard Chalhoub is affiliated with the University of Clermont Auvergne, France. Additional collaborations are acknowledged in the paper.
