Published Papers

The development and evaluation of LoRaFlexSim are documented in peer‑reviewed publications. You can access the full texts through the links below.

  • LoRaFlexSim: A Flexible LoRa/LoRaWAN Simulator – this paper introduces the simulator’s architecture, emphasising multichannel operation, device classes A/B/C, OTAA, mobility models, Adaptive Data Rate schemes and per‑node energy accounting. It validates the simulator against reference frameworks and presents several use cases.
  • Implementing LoRaWAN End‑Device Classes A, B, and C in LoRaFlexSim – this follow‑up study details the implementation of LoRaWAN device classes within LoRaFlexSim and analyses their performance across different scenarios.

These papers provide in‑depth explanations of the simulator’s design choices and validation methodology. They also outline potential research directions that can be explored using LoRaFlexSim.
